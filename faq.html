Frequently Asked Questions?
Who should attend this course?

You are currently in a business analysis role and feel ill-equipped for the role

You are an aspiring Business Analyst from any industry who wants to transition into tech to become a Product Manager

Recent graduates that want to start their careers as Business Analyst

You are planning to write a certification exam in business analysis

How long is the training?

The training is for 12 weeks which includes 4 weeks of intensive learning and 8 weeks of project work, mentoring and a wrap with interview guide and job search tips.

Is there any certification offered at the end of the program?

Yes, you will get a verified certificate of attendance after successful completion of the program.

Do I need any prerequisite knowledge before enrollling on the program?

You will need a good internet connection to connect online inorder to take your training

You will need a device, preferably a laptop to do your projects and assignments

And most importantly you will need the passion to excel.
